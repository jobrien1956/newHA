46ers

46 Adirondack peaks over 4,000ft

Climbing the 46s with hopefully 15 or so hikes

My objective in creating this page was to accumlate as much as I could about the 46ers in an easy to follow guide for actually doing the hikes. Where I could I have also linked each of the hikes to the AllTrails.com page for each hike.

The 'Intro' and 'Plan of Attack' generally are from an excellent article 'How to Become an Adirondack 46er' in Backpacker magazine by Erik Johnson from July 2, 2015

As I make my way thru the 46ers I will be updating the list below with the results of my trips and pictures on the peaks and at various spots along the way.
